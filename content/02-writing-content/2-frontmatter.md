---
title: "Frontmatter"
---

Every markdown file should start with a YAML frontmatter block to define its metadata.

```yaml
---
title: "My Awesome Post"
description: "A short summary for SEO."
date: 2024-01-01
---
```

- `title`: Used for the H1 tag and the `<title>` tag.
- `description`: Used for the meta description.
- `date`: Used for sorting if the site is in "blog" mode.
