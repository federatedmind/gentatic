Today we are excited to announce a massive overhaul to the **Blog Mode** in GenTatic. We've added a ton of new features designed to make your content shine.

## New Features

1. **Cover Images**: You can now specify a `coverImage` in your frontmatter to display a rich thumbnail on the homepage and a beautiful hero banner at the top of your articles.
2. **Authors**: Give credit where credit is due with the new `author` frontmatter field.
3. **Tags**: Organize your content with tags. You can now click on tags to see all related posts.
4. **Estimated Reading Time**: We now automatically calculate the estimated reading time for your articles.
5. **Featured Posts**: Pin your best content to the top of the homepage using `featured: true`.
6. **Dark Mode**: Seamlessly switch between light and dark themes with the new toggle in the header.

Enjoy the new features!
